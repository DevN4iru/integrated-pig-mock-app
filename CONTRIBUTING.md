# Contributing to Pigstep

Pigstep is proprietary software.

Contributions are accepted only from authorized developers, contractors, or collaborators approved by the project owner.

By contributing code, documentation, design, configuration, or other materials to this repository, you agree that your contribution may be used, modified, distributed, deployed, sublicensed, and commercialized as part of Pigstep under the repository's proprietary license or under separate customer/developer agreements.

Do not contribute code or assets that you do not have the right to submit.

Do not include third-party proprietary code, secrets, credentials, private keys, database dumps, customer data, or unauthorized licensed materials.

All contributors must preserve confidentiality and must not share source code or project materials outside authorized Pigstep work.
